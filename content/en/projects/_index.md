Our group is involved in several key projects aimed at developing new therapeutic strategies for aging, cancer, and neurodegenerative diseases.

{{< image src="images/projects/p1.jpg" style=""  >}}

### Chemical Probe Development

  Developing novel chemical probes for probing protein functions, with applications in drug discovery and targeted therapy for cancer and neurodegenerative diseases.

### Senescence and Inflammaging

  Exploring the molecular mechanisms underlying cellular senescence and the senescence-associated secretory phenotype (SASP) in aging and disease, focusing on therapeutic approaches for modulating these pathways.
